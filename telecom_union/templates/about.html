Our Mission!

We are an organization that is passionately committed to promoting the well-being and interests of all those involved in the telecommunications industry. Our mission is to serve as a unifying force, advocating for the rights and prosperity of telecommunications professionals, employees, and stakeholders.

Who we are?

Telecommunication Trade Union is a dynamic and inclusive organization that brings together individuals and entities from various sectors of the telecommunications industry. Established with the vision of fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and advocacy, we are driven by a shared passion for innovation, progress, and equitable treatment within the field.
